What is this about?

this site is made to help disc golf tournament directors find and request parks where they can hold their events. it’s way easier than trying to call the city or fill out papers by hand. we’re trying to make the process smooth and stress-free.

What We Do

We are a digital platform designed to simplify park and disc golf course reservations across municipalities. Whether you're planning a casual game with friends or organizing a tournament, our system helps you find, reserve, and manage public disc golf spaces efficiently.

Who We Are

This service is a collaboration between local municipalities and the disc golf community. Through crowdsourced contributions and official park data, we keep our course listings, layouts, and maintenance schedules up-to-date and accurate.

How It Works

  • Municipalities  provide verified park and course information.
  • Users  can view available courses, make reservations, and submit updates.
  • Admins  moderate the data and maintain system reliability.
  • Reservations  are managed through our system, where users can confirm event dates and track their bookings.

Community Contributions

Our users play a key role in keeping the system current. From updating course maps to reporting closures or changes, contributions are tracked in a versioned log to ensure data accuracy.

Our Goal

We aim to grow the disc golf community by making public spaces more accessible, discoverable, and manageable. This platform encourages transparency, collaboration, and fun — all in support of one of the world’s fastest-growing sports.
